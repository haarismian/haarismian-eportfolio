Unit 4

Learning Objectives

  • Explore the use of regular expressions and recursion in creating software solutions.
  • Investigate the effects of using regular expressions and recursion on system security.
  • Recommend the most suitable approaches for given security requirements/risk appetites.
  • Explain how and when to use regular expressions in solutions.
  • Describe how and when to use recursion in solutions.
  • Discuss the security implications of both approaches (regular expressions and recursion).

Reflections of the activity and course learning objectives

This unit provides a fundamental understanding that I believe is critical for my academic progression and future career. Regular expressions and recursion are core concepts in programming, often underappreciated yet vital for crafting efficient software solutions. Understanding these concepts and their application will enhance my ability to develop more sophisticated and performance-efficient software, which is a crucial attribute of a skilled software engineer.

Furthermore, the focus on system security implications resulting from the use of regular expressions and recursion is incredibly valuable. With the increasing number of security threats and breaches in today's digital landscape, having this knowledge will undoubtedly equip me with the skills to ensure the software I develop is secure and resilient. These aspects, combined with the capacity to align the chosen approaches with specific security requirements or risk appetites, will be a significant advantage in my future career as a software engineer. Not only will I be able to create effective software solutions, but I'll also be capable of ensuring that they are safe, reliable, and meet the users' needs. Thus, the lessons from this unit will have a profound impact on my education and future career objectives.
