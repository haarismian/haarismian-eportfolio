Learning Objectives

  • Introduction to Qualitative Data Analysis: Explore various methods for analyzing, interpreting, and presenting results from qualitative data.
  • Critical Assessment of Analysis Methods: Discuss the advantages and potential biases of different qualitative analysis techniques, emphasizing the importance of maintaining objectivity.
  • Data Presentation Techniques: Learn about different charts and graphical tools available to effectively present qualitative and quantitative data in a clear and understandable manner.

Reflections of the activity and course learning objectives

Week 9 has enhanced my skills in handling qualitative data, crucial for the exploratory aspects of my research. The discussions on the subjective nature of qualitative analysis and the techniques to minimize bias were particularly enlightening. Learning about coding responses and the use of specialized software for data analysis will greatly aid in categorizing and interpreting the complex data I anticipate collecting. Moreover, the focus on effectively presenting results using charts and graphs will be invaluable for communicating my findings clearly. This unit has equipped me to better analyze and convey the nuances of qualitative data, ensuring that my research conclusions are both rigorous and compelling.
