Unit 12

Learning Objectives

  • Define and understand the fundamental concepts of project management, including the project life cycle and methodologies.
  • Identify and assess the impacts of risks and uncertainties on projects, and learn how to develop comprehensive risk management and change management plans.
  • Implement effective strategies to control risk and manage changes within projects to maintain quality, cost, and timeline, while leveraging potential opportunities.

Reflections of the activity and course learning objectives

Unit 12 provided valuable insights into project management fundamentals and risk handling in technology projects. Understanding project life cycles and various methodologies has been enlightening (Desmond, 2023). Learning to identify potential issues proactively and adapt strategies to maintain project momentum has been crucial (Jamison, 2023). This knowledge will be instrumental in my capstone project and future career. The unit has equipped me with practical tools for navigating complexities in the tech industry (ProjectManager, 2023). Understanding the software development life cycle (SDLC) and its phases has further enhanced my preparedness for real-world challenges (AWS, 2023). Additionally, exploring how AI might transform project management in the future has broadened my perspective on the field's evolution (Caster and Kirn, 2023).
