Learning Objectives

  • Define and understand the fundamental concepts of project management, including the project life cycle and methodologies.
  • Identify and assess the impacts of risks and uncertainties on projects, and learn how to develop comprehensive risk management and change management plans.
  • Implement effective strategies to control risk and manage changes within projects to maintain quality, cost, and timeline, while leveraging potential opportunities.

Reflections of the activity and course learning objectives

Unit 12 really opened my eyes to the nuts and bolts of project management and the tricky business of handling risks in tech projects. Getting the grasp on how project life cycles work and the different ways to approach them has been very enlightening. I feel more prepared to tackle the complexities that might come my way and lead my projects to a successful finish. This unit has given me real, practical tools I can use once I step out of the classroom and into the tech world.
