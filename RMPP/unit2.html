Learning Objectives

  • Formulate and Refine Research Questions: Develop skills in identifying and refining a research question that addresses a significant gap in existing knowledge, beginning with an initial idea and iterating through a process of critical questioning.
  • Conduct and Present a Literature Review: Learn to effectively conduct a literature review within a chosen area of study, which involves identifying relevant scholarly sources, synthesizing current knowledge, and pinpointing gaps in the research. Gain skills in presenting the findings of the literature review clearly and cohesively.
  • Prepare a Research Proposal: Understand the components of a research proposal and how to assemble them into a coherent document. This includes the creation of a structured argument that aligns the research question, literature review, and proposed methods to demonstrate the feasibility and relevance of the research.

Reflections of the activity and course learning objectives

Reflecting on Unit 2, I find that the skills and knowledge I've started to develop are crucial for any aspiring researcher. The idea that a good researcher is one "struggling to find out what the right questions might be" really resonates with me. It shifts my focus from seeking definite answers to embracing the process of inquiry itself. Learning how to formulate and refine a research question has shown me the importance of clarity and precision in research, ensuring that every inquiry made is purposeful and grounded in existing knowledge gaps. The literature review process, on the other hand, has opened up a vast landscape of scholarship that I need to navigate effectively, teaching me not only to gather information but also to critically evaluate and organize it to build a compelling argument. Preparing the research proposal has been particularly transformative; it has been about learning to present my ideas in a structured manner that convincingly bridges theory and method, which I believe is a critical skill for any academic endeavor. Overall, this unit has been an integral step in shaping my approach to academic research, emphasizing thorough preparation and ethical consideration in every phase of the research process.
