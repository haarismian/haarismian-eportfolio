Unit 11

Learning Objectives

  • Analyze and refine my learning process through reflections, enabling me to tailor my ongoing professional development effectively.
  • Complete the Professional Skills Matrix, assessing my skills against my professional goals and the outcomes from my program to guide future learning and career pathways. I admittedly did not complete this unfortunately though I am regularly mappnig out my professional skills
  • Develop a comprehensive action plan based on the Professional Skills Matrix to support continuous professional development and prepare for career advancement.

Reflections of the activity and course learning objectives

As I approach the end of my Master's program, Unit 11 has been instrumental in catalyzing my professional growth through the use of my e-Portfolio. Reflecting on my learning process has not only solidified my understanding of my academic and personal development but has also sharpened my ability to apply these insights in a professional setting. Learning about the Professional Skills Matrix was particularly enlightening; it allowed me to visually map out my competencies against my career aspirations, making the gaps and strengths in my skills evident. This unit has essentially provided me with a clear, actionable plan that I anticipate will guide my continuous professional development well beyond my graduation, ensuring that I remain adaptable and competitive in the tech industry (Miller and Morgaine, 2007; Valamis, 2023; BCS, 2024).
