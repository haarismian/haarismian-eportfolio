Research Methods and Professional Practice

Learning Objectives

  • Acquire a comprehensive understanding of research principles and methods across various disciplines, enhancing abilities in critical analysis, reflection, and application of the scientific method to research designs and strategies.
  • Develop key research competencies, including data collection and analysis, fostering the ability to design, execute, and critically evaluate independent research projects.
  • Appraise professional, legal, social, cultural, and ethical considerations in computing, applying principles of academic investigation to critically assess existing literature, research methodologies, and design in the context of a specific computing research topic.
